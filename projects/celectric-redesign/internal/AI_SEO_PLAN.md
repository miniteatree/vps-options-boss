# Celectric Redesign - AI Assisted SEO Plan

## Goal
Use AI as an internal assistant to improve SEO workflow without allowing unreviewed auto-publishing.

## Principles
- AI generates drafts, not final truth.
- Human review is required before publish.
- Technical specifications must not be invented.
- AI should work from structured product data where possible.

## Initial AI Use Cases
- Generate SEO title
- Generate meta description
- Rewrite short product summary for readability
- Draft category introduction
- Draft brand page introduction
- Suggest FAQs
- Suggest keywords

## Priority Pages
1. Product pages
2. Category pages
3. Brand pages
4. Industry / solution pages

## Workflow
1. Product or page data is saved
2. Admin clicks AI assist action
3. AI generates draft content
4. Admin reviews and edits
5. Final content is approved and published

## Guardrails
- Do not invent specifications
- Do not invent certifications
- Do not claim unsupported compatibility
- Keep product naming and model information exact

## Future Enhancements
- Content completeness scoring
- Internal linking suggestions
- SEO issue flagging
- Keyword clustering suggestions
