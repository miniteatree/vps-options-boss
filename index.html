Bottom Line

  • Cheapest simple route: Exabytes NVMe C2 at RM60/month, keep images/admin where they are first.
  • Chosen route: Exabytes VPS + Cloudflare at RM150/month for 300GB storage.
  • If comparing non-Exabytes VPS: AWS Lightsail looks attractive on entry price, while DigitalOcean and Vultr are solid but not clearly cheaper after converting to RM.
  • If images stay on local VPS disk: cost looks okay at first, but storage growth becomes the long-term problem.
